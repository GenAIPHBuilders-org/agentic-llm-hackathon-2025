Program Timeline

Key dates and milestones for the Gen AI Builders PH program

Begin Hackathon - Registration Opens

March 20, 2024

Official start of the Gen AI Builders PH program. Team registration opens for all interested participants.

Register Now

Registration Deadline

March 30, 2024

Last day to register your team for the program. Make sure to complete your application before this date.

Kick-off Event

April 10, 2024

Official kick-off event for all registered teams. Introduction to the program, mentors, and resources.

Session 1: Agents Workshop

April 20, 2024

First technical workshop focusing on building AI agents and their applications.

Session 2: Fine-tuning Workshop

April 30, 2024

Workshop on fine-tuning large language models for specific applications and use cases.

Session 3: Multi-agentic Workshop

May 10, 2024

Advanced workshop on building multi-agent systems and collaborative AI architectures.

Pitch Deck Submission

May 20, 2024

Deadline for teams to submit their project pitch decks for initial evaluation.

Stage II Selection

May 30, 2024

Announcement of teams advancing to Stage II of the program based on pitch deck evaluations.

Resource Access Deadline

June 10, 2024

Last day for teams to request and access additional resources for their projects.

Demo Submission

June 20, 2024

Final deadline for teams to submit their project demonstrations and documentation.

Demo Day & Finals

June 30, 2024

Final event where teams present their projects to judges and the community. Winners will be announced.
