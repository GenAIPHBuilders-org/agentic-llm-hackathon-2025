Program Timeline

Key dates and milestones for the Gen AI Builders PH program

January 15, 2024

Program Announcement

Official launch of the Gen AI Builders PH program and call for applications.

February 1, 2024

Application Deadline

Last day to submit applications for the program. Team registration closes at midnight.

February 15, 2024

Team Announcement

Selected teams are announced and invited to the kickoff event.

March 1, 2024

Kickoff Event

Official program kickoff with workshops, mentorship matching, and project planning.

March 15, 2024

First Milestone

Teams submit their project proposals and initial prototypes for feedback.

April 15, 2024

Midterm Presentations

Teams present their progress and receive feedback from mentors and judges.

May 15, 2024

Final Milestone

Teams submit their final projects for evaluation.

June 1, 2024

Demo Day

Final project demonstrations and presentations to judges, mentors, and industry partners.

June 15, 2024

Awards Ceremony

Announcement of winners and awarding of prizes and recognitions.
